Poke Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

For the Base:

  • 2 cups cooked sushi rice (or brown rice, quinoa, or greens)

For the Protein:

  • 200g sushi-grade tuna or salmon (diced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger
  • Optional: a pinch of chili flakes

For the Toppings:

  • 1 avocado (sliced or diced)
  • 1/2 cucumber (thinly sliced or diced)
  • 1/2 cup edamame (cooked and shelled)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup seaweed salad (optional)
  • 2 tbsp pickled ginger
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (white or black)
  • 1 green onion (thinly sliced)

For the Drizzle (Optional):

  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp sriracha sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp lime juice

Instructions

  1. Cook the Base
  2. Prepare your sushi rice (or alternative base) and let it cool slightly. Prepare the Protein
  3. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, honey, grated ginger, and chili flakes. Add the diced fish and toss gently. Let marinate in the fridge for about 15 minutes. Make the Drizzle (Optional)
  4. Mix mayonnaise, sriracha, and lime juice in a small bowl. Adjust spice level to taste. Assemble the Bowl
  5. Divide the rice or base into two bowls. Arrange marinated fish, avocado, cucumber, edamame, carrots, seaweed salad, and pickled ginger on top. Garnish and Serve
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds and green onion over the bowl. Drizzle with the spicy mayo, if desired. Enjoy
  7. Serve immediately with chopsticks for an authentic experience!
